# MAD9022 Deliverables

This section shows all the graded deliverables for MAD9022. Every deliverable has a list of requirements, instructions for submission, and the week when it is due. For the exact due dates and times for your section, see the assignment page in BS LMS.

Late Penalty

Program late penalty for deliverables.

  • Up to one day late -10%
  • Up to two days late -20%
  • Three to ten days late -30%
  • After 10 days grade of zero

# Verbal Quiz Questions

Worth 20% of Final Grade

At the beginning of most classes, several questions will be asked based on the weekly readings and videos, the current assignment work, and prior lectures.

Each question correctly answered is worth 4%. Answer 5 questions over the course of the semester to earn the full 20%.

# Hybrid Exercises

Worth 15% of Final Grade

The Hybrid exercises are small exercises meant to be completed outside of class hours as part of the asynchronous self-directed portion of the course. You will be provided with references to use to complete each exercise. There are four of these exercises spread across the semester.

Don't wait until the day before they are due to start. You will need time too research and understand the material before completing each of the exercises.

Hybrid Exercise List

# Timed Exercises

Worth 15% of Final Grade

Over the course of the semester there will be five exercises that need to be completed during class time. Each exercise will have a limited amount of time to complete.

The exercises must be completed alone but you can use any online resource.

The topic for each exercise, as well as references to prepare, will be announced the week proceeding each exercise.

See BS LMS for the dates of the exercises.
